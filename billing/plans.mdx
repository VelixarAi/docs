## Free tier

The free tier is designed for prototyping and evaluation:
- 100 memory stores per month
- 500 retrievals per month
- 30 requests per minute
- No overage allowed — requests are rejected at the limit

## Paid tiers

All paid tiers include:
- Overage billing (you're never hard-blocked)
- Auto-extraction pipeline
- Semantic deduplication
- Conflict resolution

Upgrade anytime from the [dashboard](https://app.velixar.ai/pricing).
