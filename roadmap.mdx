## Shipped Recently

<Steps>
  <Step title="Cognitive Architecture (Exocortex)">
    Complete cognitive architecture with encrypted vector storage, knowledge graph, immutable audit trail, automatic deep consolidation, and salience-based recall.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Knowledge Graph">
    Content-addressed directed graph that automatically extracts entities and relationships from memories. Graph traversal enriches every recall with connected context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Envelope Encryption">
    Workspace-scoped AES-256-GCM envelope encryption on all memory embeddings via AWS KMS. Zero-knowledge architecture — your data is unreadable, even to us.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Immutable Audit Trail">
    Hash-chained, tamper-evident logging of every memory operation. Cryptographic proof of what your AI knew and when.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deep Consolidation">
    Automatic synthesis of related memories into higher-order knowledge on a recurring schedule. Hierarchical abstraction from facts to topics to domains.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Org Memory Promotion">
    Promote workspace memories to the organization level with full lineage tracking. Knowledge graph nodes and audit entries follow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Per-Workspace Collections">
    Optional isolated vector collections per workspace for TB-scale deployments with complete data separation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Team Workspaces">
    Shared memory namespaces for teams. Invite members, manage permissions with RBAC, and share org-wide knowledge across your team's AI applications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="MCP Server on Official Registry">
    Published to the official MCP Registry as io.github.VelixarAi/memory. Works with Claude Desktop, Kiro, Cursor, Windsurf, and any MCP client.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-Extraction Pipeline">
    Automatically extract memories from conversations with semantic dedup (cosine > 0.85), conflict resolution, and smart tier routing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stripe Metered Billing">
    Usage-based billing with overage support. Free tier hard caps, paid tiers allow overages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Python & JavaScript SDKs">
    Official clients on PyPI and npm with full TypeScript support.
  </Step>
  <Step title="VS Code Extension">
    Velixar memory recall directly in your editor. Available on the VS Code Marketplace.
  </Step>
</Steps>
